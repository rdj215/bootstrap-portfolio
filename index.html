About Me

Greetings! My name is Ryan Jamison and I am currently searching for a technical position that will allow me to build fluid full stack web applications while continuing to learn and develop my craft. I recently graduated from the University of Pennsylvania's Immersive Full Stack Web Developer Bootcamp and have worked with some of the latest technologies including JavaScript, CSS, HTML, Node.js, React.js, MySQL & MySQL Workbench, MongoDB & Mongoose, Git, Visual Studio, and Bootstrap.

I own a cleaning business which I started at the age of 20 which has taught me many valuable lessons that have transcended beyond being a business owner. Being an entrepreneur has taught me how to effectively communicate, build relationships, become risk averse, and to be a continual learner.

In addition to running a business I am active in local politics serving as a Committee Person in the 50th ward (highest voting ward in the city of Philadelphia) and a member of several non-profits. This allows me to engage with a diverse group of individuals including elected officials, stakeholders, and residents from across the city to help create tangible change in the city.

I'm confident my unique work and personal experiences along with my technical skills would make me an asset to your team. In my spare time I enjoy reading, working out, and spending time with family. I love to watch action and sci-fi movies when I get a chance to watch television which are rare these days.
